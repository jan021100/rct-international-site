Our Events

This page lists the races and special events we do together as a team—road, gravel, MTB, running, and tri. For big race weekends we coordinate travel, lodging, and support so members from Prague, Germany, and beyond can meet up and compete as one crew.

Upcoming team races

Road • Germany

Škoda Velotour — Eschborn–Frankfurt

The amateur Velotour runs on the same day as the WorldTour classic. The city turns into a cycling festival—perfect for an RCT meetup and a long team weekend. The event takes place annually on May 1.

  • Team trip: Frankfurt area (car/rail), shared logistics & group dinner.
  • Multiple course options (Velotour variants announced each season).
  • Open to all levels—ride together, meet the DE chapter.

MTB / Gravel • Czechia

ČT Author Cup — Bedřichov

Iconic Jizera Mountains mass‑start race. Our Prague crew turns this into a proper weekend: cabin share, pre‑ride, race, then a celebratory meal. The 2025 edition is on 11 October 2025 (Bedřichov).

  • Team trip: shared transport & lodging from Prague.
  • Fast mass‑start vibe; MTB and gravel both welcome.
  • Family‑friendly weekend atmosphere.

Want something added? Members can propose a race and help host the team trip (transport, rooms, dinner spot). We’ll list it here and rally the crew.

Travel & lodging

We group people by departure city (Prague / Frankfurt region / others) and share rides or trains. Lodging is usually a shared apartment or cabins close to the start.

Course & logistics

Someone pre‑checks routes, timing, bib pickup, start pens, and a post‑race meet point. Expect all info in the WhatsApp group.

All levels welcome

If it’s your first race, we’ll help with pacing, kit, nutrition, and nerves. If you’re hunting PRs, we’ll help you line up with similar goals.
