Ride. Train. Race. Belong.

RCT Cycling is for everyone: from relaxed weekend riders to performance-driven racers. We’re a friendly crew that values friendships and community first, and we also create space for structure, progress, and pinning on a number. If you want a welcoming group to ride with or a team to race with, you’ll fit right in.

RCT Racing Squad

For riders who want more structure and competition, the RCT Racing Squad provides a pathway into local and regional races - from road and crits to cyclocross and endurance challenges. We set targets together, support each other on and off the bike, and plan a few team highlight events each season.

  • Group training focus and racing calendar
  • Shared logistics for selected events
  • Knowledge sharing: gear, nutrition, pacing, tactics

Team kit

Our team kit comes in two colorways: white for official events and team representation, and pink for high-visibility and training. Both come in a range of fits, from performance-focused race cuts to more relaxed options, so you can choose what suits your riding style. The kit is paired with black bib shorts for a clean, unified look. Accessories like wind vests, jackets, long-sleeve jerseys, and one-piece suits are also available. You can view the full range in our shop page and order individually or join a group order we arrange from time to time.
