What we’re about

RCT International brings together people who share a passion for endurance sports. Our community blends the energy of group rides and runs with the professionalism of a well-organized sports team and the creativity of a modern lifestyle brand.

From local meet-ups to international events, we create opportunities to ride, run, race, and connect. Whether you’re chasing personal bests or just enjoying the journey, RCT is about pushing limits, discovering new places, and making it unforgettable.

Chapters

Our most active and largest chapter, based in the heart of Europe. All sports are represented here by a young and dynamic community of students, young professionals, and passionate athletes. Multiple weekly events, regular race participation, and a thriving social scene make it the beating heart of RCT, especially as this is where most of our activities and projects are currently led.

The birthplace of RCT, located in the scenic Hochtaunus region. While there are currently no regular events, a small but close-knit community stays active through regional and cross-chapter rides, races, and social activities. We’re working to grow the local scene, with plans to establish regular rides, runs, and youth-focused programs in the future. All set against some of Germany’s most beautiful cycling and running routes.
